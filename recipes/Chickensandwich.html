High Protein Chicken Sandwich

A juicy grilled chicken sandwich packed with protein, fresh veggies, and a tangy yogurt sauce for a delicious, healthy meal.

This high-protein grilled chicken sandwich is a perfect balance of flavor and nutrition. The tender, seasoned chicken breast provides a substantial source of protein, while the creamy Greek yogurt sauce adds a tangy zest without unnecessary calories. Fresh veggies like lettuce, tomato, onion, and avocado not only enhance the taste but also add vital nutrients and fiber. The whole-grain or sourdough bun complements the ingredients, making the sandwich hearty yet light. Perfect for a quick lunch or dinner, this sandwich is easy to make, satisfying, and ideal for fitness enthusiasts or anyone seeking a nutritious, tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6 oz)
  • 1 whole-grain or sourdough bun
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 2-3 red onion slices
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder (to taste)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Grill the chicken on medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  3. Mix Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice for the sauce.
  4. Toast the bun lightly, if desired.
  5. Assemble the sandwich with lettuce, tomato, onion, grilled chicken, yogurt sauce, and avocado.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
