High Protein Breakfast Burrito

Start your day with a flavorful, protein-packed breakfast burrito to keep you full and energized.

This high-protein breakfast burrito is the perfect way to start your day with a balance of flavor and nutrition. Packed with fluffy scrambled eggs, savory turkey sausage, hearty black beans, and vibrant veggies, it offers a satisfying mix of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. A sprinkle of cheese adds a creamy touch, while fresh spinach and salsa bring brightness and a hint of spice. Wrapped in a warm whole wheat tortilla, this burrito is both delicious and portable, making it an ideal breakfast for busy mornings or a hearty post-workout meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla
  • 4 large egg whites (or 2 whole eggs + 2 whites)
  • ½ cup cooked black beans
  • 2 turkey sausage links (sliced) or 2 oz cooked ground turkey
  • ¼ cup shredded low-fat cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • ¼ cup diced bell peppers
  • ¼ cup diced onions
  • 1 handful fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp salsa or hot sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or non-stick spray

Steps

  1. Cook the Filling: Heat a skillet with olive oil or non-stick spray over medium heat. Sauté the bell peppers, onions, and spinach until softened. Add sliced turkey sausage (or ground turkey) and cook until heated through.
  2. Scramble the Eggs: Whisk the egg whites or whole eggs in a bowl. Pour them into the skillet with the vegetables and sausage. Cook until scrambled.
  3. Assemble the Burrito: Warm the tortilla in the microwave or on a skillet for a few seconds. Lay the cooked eggs, beans, and cheese in the center of the tortilla. Top with salsa or hot sauce if desired.
  4. Roll It Up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  5. Optional Toasting: Place the burrito back on the skillet for 1-2 minutes on each side to lightly toast the tortilla.
